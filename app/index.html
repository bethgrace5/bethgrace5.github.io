Hi, I'm Beth...

I’m a software developer. I started college studying math and physics. After after taking an elective class on Java, I was hooked. I could solve real business through using programming solve real problems for businesses. If I continued doing math there would be many years of theory before I would be able to get to the practical side of my education.

I currently work for a local custom software development company, Mershon Enterprises. I started as an intern in late 2012 and have worked my way up to be one of the top developers in the company. Through my experiences since I started I enjoy both learning from more experienced developers and teaching some of the more junior developers. I’ve been able to share my knowledge with others that are experiencing blockers. I am able to take a concept and explain it in simple terms so that it’s understandable to someone less experienced.

While working at Mershon Enterprises I have worked on two core projects. The first is a distributed web application designed for generalized data collection and warehousing, sort of like a small-scale ERP. The second is a platform to create virtual memorials to honor loved ones, pets, and celebrities and donate to relevant charities.
